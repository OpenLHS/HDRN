    Terms

    Term Definition
    ambulance initiated emergency department visit An emergency department visit for a given patient that was participating in another healthcare organisation visit involving paramedics just prior to this visit.
    ancillary care service delivery A service delivery that aims to support health services without directly providing a desired effect on the health status of individuals or communities.
    biological sex or gender identity information content entity An information content entity that is intended to denote a biological sex or a gender identity.
    birth statement A statement about the information surrounding the birth of a human.
    cadaver service A healthcare organization service delivery where the main entity of interest is a human cadaver that was not delivered just prior to this service.
    canadian census subdivision identifier An identifier that denotes a municipality (as determined by provincial/territorial legislation) or an area treated as a municipal equivalent for statistical purposes (e.g., Indian reserves, Indian settlements and unorganized territories).
    canadian facility address
    canadian first-order administrative region identifier An identifier that denotes a Canadian province or a Canadian territory.
    canadian forward sorting area code An identifier that denotes a geographical region on which is based the first three characters of a Canadian postal code.
    canadian postal address A postal address that can be used to help the delivery of something in Canada.
    canadian postal code A postal code information that is about some geographical region in Canada.
    civil birth record A state sanctioned statement about the information surrounding the birth of a human.
    civil death record A state sanctioned statement about the information surrounding the death of a human.
    clinical visit ending determination A healthcare procedure where the responsible healthcare worker comes to the conclusion that a clinical visit can end.
    custodial care service delivery A service delivery from an organization that aims to provide for the activities of daily living of individuals or communities.
    day inpatient surgery visit A day inpatient visit which includes a surgical procedure.
    day inpatient visit An inpatient visit that does not extend overnight.
    death statement A statement about the information surrounding the death of a human.
    diagnosis related group
    diagnostic health procedure TBD
    dispensed drug quantity A data item that is about the total quantity of a drug product that was distributed during a drug dispensing procedure.
    drug dispensing covered duration specification A directive information entity that specifies over how much time a dispensed drug quantity should be administered.
    drug dispensing drug product charge The amount paid by the payer to a pharmacy for the drug.
    drug dispensing healthcare provider charge The amount paid by the Payer to a pharmacy for dispensing a drug.
    drug dispensing procedure A healthcare procedure in which a specified quantity of a particular drug product is made available with the goal of the drug product being administered to an organism.
    drug dispensing record An information content entity that is usually part of a pharmacist information system and includes patient identification as well as drug dispensing record item.
    drug dispensing record item A data item that is part of a drug-dispensing record and describes how much of some drug products have been dispensed to a patient.
    drug dispensing record item dispensing date A process temporal characterization that is about a drug dispensing procedure.
    drug dispensing total charge The total amount charged by a healthcare organization to payers to cover a drug dispensing procedure and related drug products.
    drug dispensing total payment The total amount actually paid from all payers to cover a drug dispensing procedure and related drug products.
    drug product characterization An information content entity that characterizes a class of drug product.
    emergency department inpatient visit An emergency department visit and an inpatient visit, where there is usually limited accommodation and where the goal is to stabilise a life-threatening condition or decide whether the clinical situation requires a hospitalisation visit.
    emergency department outpatient visit An emergency department visit and an outpatient visit.
    emergency department visit A healthcare organisation clinical visit where there is expectation of providing unplanned, timely, urgent care, including handling life-threatening conditions. It is not necessarily an immediate inpatient subsequent visit.
    emergency department visit with partial treatment An emergency department visit and a visit ended by the patient and some treatment healthcare procedure was provided.
    facility address A postal address that enables an agent to locate a facility.
    facility-supported clinical visit
    facility-supported healthcare encounter A healthcare encounter during which the recipient is located in a healthcare facility at some time.
    health activity A planned process that aims to produce a truthful statement about the health status of an organism or modify it.
    health activity outcome information An information content entity that is a specified output of a health activity.
    health procedure A planned process guided by the objective of contributing to a desired effect on the health status of an organism or several organisms achieved through the treatment, diagnosis, or prevention of disease or injury. It has some components that are planned processes, including at least one that is a health activity.
    health procedure order A health procedure request that is the output of a service agreement made between a request party and a service offer party and that specifies some health procedures to be realized.
    health procedure outcome information An information content entity that is the output of a health procedure resulting process and that describes some outcomes of a health procedure.
    health procedure report An information content entity containing information about some health procedure requests and possibly the associated health procedures.
    health procedure request A request specifying the execution of some health procedures.
    health procedure resulting process A planned process that is a part of a health procedure, that has as specified input at least one health activity outcome information and aim at generating a health procedure outcome information.
    health provider An organism that is the bearer of a health provider role.
    health provider identifier An individual identifier that denotes a health provider.
    health provider role A role that inheres in an organism as a consequence of training in order to be able to participate as a performer in some health activities.
    healthcare birth visit A healthcare organization clinical visit during which a birth statement was produced regarding the birth of the main patient visit beneficiary.
    healthcare facility TBD
    healthcare facility identifier An identifier that denotes a building administered by a healthcare organization for the purpose of providing healthcare to a patient or patient population.
    healthcare organization An organisation that bears a healthcare organisation role.
    healthcare organization clinical encounter A temporally-connected healthcare organization clinical visit that aims to improve, maintain or restore the health of some participating organism.
    healthcare organization clinical visit A healthcare organisation service delivery that aims at providing a desired effect on the health status of a specified organism. It is usually composed of health procedures and possibly associated ancillary processes.
    healthcare organization clinical visit finish charaterisation A temporal characterisation that is about the finish of a healthcare organisation clinical visit
    healthcare organization clinical visit start charaterisation A temporal characterisation that is about the start of a healthcare organisation clinical visit
    healthcare organization identifier An identifier that denotes a healthcare organization.
    healthcare organization role A role that inheres in an organisation and is realized by providing some healthcare service delivery.
    healthcare organization service delivery A service delivery under the responsibility of a healthcare organisation. It stems from an agreement between a requesting agent and a healthcare organization.
    healthcare procedure A health procedure that is part of a healthcare service delivery and that is performed by a healthcare worker while realizing its health care worker role.
    healthcare provider A health provider who is a member of a healthcare organisation.
    healthcare provider identifier An identifier that denotes a healthcare provider.
    healthcare service organism specification A service agreement specification identifying an organism for whom a healthcare service delivery aims at providing a desired effect to its health status.
    healthcare visit of expiration A healthcare organization clinical visit during which a death statement was produced regarding the death of the main patient visit beneficiary.
    hospitalization An inpatient visit located in a hospital facility requested by a physician, that may extends overnight.
    human biological sex information content entity An information content entity that denotes the biological sex of a human.
    human birth date A process temporal specification that is about the birth date of an homo sapiens.
    human clinical visit specified patient
    human death date A process temporal specification that is about the date of death of an homo sapiens.
    human identifier An identifier that denotes a particular human being.
    immediate inpatient subsequent visit An immediate subsequent visit healthcare organisation visit for a given patient that was participating in another healthcare organisation visit for the same organisation just prior to this visit.
    immediate subsequent visit
    immediate subsequent visit in same organization An immediate subsequent visit where both visits are under the responsability of the same healthcare organisation.
    informational entity A generically dependent continuant that is a building block of information or a combination thereof.
    informational filler An informational entity that fills an informational slot.
    informational slot An informational entity that can be filled by an informational filler.
    inpatient visit A healthcare organisation clinical visit where there is expectation of providing support for activities of daily living (such as eating and bathing) and accommodation for the patient.
    inpatient visit from an external location an inpatient visit that is not immedately following another healthcare organisation clinical visit in the same organisation, and is not a healthcare birth visit
    outpatient diagnostic visit an outpatient visit which includes a diagnostic procedure
    outpatient surgery visit an outpatient visit which includes a surgical procedure
    outpatient visit A healthcare organisation clinical visit where the patient physically attends to a healthcare facility, but where there is no expectation of providing support for activities of daily living (such as eating and bathing) nor accommodation for the patient.
    physician identifier A health provider identifier that is used to denote a person authorised to act as a physician.
    populational healthcare service A healthcare service that aims at providing a desired effect on the health status of a community.
    postal address city An identifier that denotes a city as part of a postal address.
    postal address country An identifier that denotes a country is part of a postal address.
    primary health insurance beneficiary human A human that has a role of primary health insurance benificiary role
    primary health insurance beneficiary human role A role that is borne by an human that is the primary beneficiary of a health insurance plan and so is responsible for its associated contract.
    prior visit category to an inpatient visit An information content entity describing the type of visit that occured prior to an immediate inpatient subsequent visit.
    process temporal characterization A temporal specification that is about the occupation of a temporal interval by some processes.
    provincial health insurance primary residence statement beginning
    public provincial health identifier An identifier that denotes a public provincial health insurance record.
    public provincial health insurance primary residence statement
    public provincial health insurance record
    request An information content entity representing the will of a person to have something done and specifications for the latter.
    service agreement negotiation A planned process of interaction between a request party and a service offer party having as specified input some request and some service offer and that aims to generate as output a service agreement.
    service agreement specification An information content entity that specifies a service agreement made between a request party and a service offer party. A service agreement specification can direct some processes that correspond to what has been agreed between the parties.
    service delivery A planned process that aims at fulfilling the commitments of a service agreement.
    service offer An information content entity describing the processes a person or an organization is willing to perform, usually for a compensation.
    state sanctioned statement A statement whom agent considering it true is a state.
    statement An informational entity that is considered valid by some agent at some point in time. (TBD)
    statement validity beginning characterization
    statement validity ending characterization
    statement validity temporal characterization A temporal characterization specifying a temporal region during which a statement is considered valid.
    still born healthcare service A healthcare organisation visit where the main entity of interest is a still born.
    surgical health procedure TBD
    temporal characterization An information content entity that is about a one-dimension temporal region.
    triage-only emergency department visit An emergency department visit with a triage encounter but no further encounter.
    visit diagnosis related group code An identifier that denotes a diagnosis related group.
    visit discharge specification A directive information entity that aims at directing what health procedures or healthcare organization services are recommended after this visit.
    visit discharge specification for home A visit discharge specification for a given clinical visit that has no visit transfer specification.
    visit ended by the patient A healthcare organization clinical visit ended at the initiative of the patient without physician approval.
    visit ended by the patient without notification A visit ended by the patient without notifying the healthcare organization.
    visit transfer specification A visit discharge specification that aims at directing to which healthcare organisation the patient should be transferred after the end of the current visit.
    visit transfer specification category An information content entity that is part of a visit transfer specification and is about the type of healthcare organization specified.
    walk-in clinic visit an outpatient visit where there is expectation of providing unplanned, timely care, but excluding handling life-threatening conditions
